American Green Power Corporation is a privately owned Export Trading Company (ETC) based in Washington, DC. Our objective is to support U.S. manufacturers of renewable energy equipment and systems by researching and developing export markets in Eastern Europe, and by finding potential buyers and investors there. Cultural, social, legal, and economic differences make exporting to this region a challenge for USA companies. We thoroughly understand this region’s languages and culture, and carefully tailor our sales and marketing efforts for each country. This includes payment, shipping, customs and pricing strategies.

American Green Power Corporation has exclusive agreements in Eastern Europe with licensed electrical companies for the installation and maintenance of renewable energy equipment and systems. Our goal is to help Eastern European customers access America’s excellent renewable energy products, while helping US suppliers to grow by safely expanding their sales into Eastern Europe.
