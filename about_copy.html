American Green Power Corporation is privately owned Export Trade Company (ETC) based in Washington, District of Columbia. Our objective is to support U.S. Manufacturer of renewable energy by researching and marketing in Eastern Europe for potential buyer and investors. Cultural, social, legal and economic differences make exporting a challenge for USA manufacturers. We have knowledge of the language and culture, also we tailor our sales and marketing with effort for each country. The same goes for payment, shipment, custom and pricing.
American Green Power Corporation has exclusive agreement with licensed electrical companies for installing and maintaining systems of renewable energy. Our goal is to help customers of Eastern Europe to get high standards and good quality product made by USA manufacturers and on other hand to help an USA manufacturers extend they business to Eastern Europe by voiding exporting challenges and have more time to concentrate on the growth of the business.
